Who am I?
Hi, my name is dylan powell and I am a senior graphic design student at Umass Dartmouth. I am from Boston Massachusetts and currently work as a freelance designer as well a security guard. Over the years Ive tried to study and learn as many different facets of design as I can. I started with learning the basics of raster and vector imaging but over the years have branched out into photography and UI/UX design. With the marketplace for technology constantly changing I am hoping to stake my claim by entering the work force with a broad range of skills and talents that will make me a better designer as a whole.
What do I do?
As a UI/UX designer, I am jack of many trades in the best of ways. I take a broad set of skills and apply them in a practical way. I believe this well rounded knowledge will allow me to be an asset to your team as I will be able to effectively communicate between different groups and bridge gaps across the workplace.
